CO Across the Nation

Across the nation, CO AQI level are actually quite low, due to improving technologies that have enabled more efficient combustion. In the graph below, not all the AQI levels were below 7 in 2015, with the mean around 4.

Looking at the distribution across the country, Colorado, Arizona and Utah once again have the highest pollution AQI levels. This time, Florida and Alaska stand out as the other states with high AQI.

The distribution of daily AQI averages is also commendable. The majority of AQI values stay below 20 and even the highest peak in Florida does not go above 50, which is considered the ‘moderate’ classification.

CO by Source

Again, we see that the transportation sector has seen the biggest decrease of approximately 23100 ppm, mostly due to better technology allowing more efficient combustion in vehicles.
